Mandelbrot

HomeProgramsMandelbrot

This is an explorative tool for the Mandelbrot set written in Rust and JavaScript. Click "Instructions/Options" for usage.

Zooming in to the edge of the black region produces the most interesting images, which can be saved from the "Instructions/Options" menu. Example images are below. Zooming in far enough will pixelate due to system constraints on representing incredibly small decimals.

Note: the Firefox setting "resistFingerprinting" can prevent this app from rendering correctly.

Instructions:

To zoom in, click and drag the mouse over the image to draw a selection box and click inside it. To pan around, drag in the image while holding shift.

Auto Zoom:

If a selection box exists, "Zoom In" goes there, otherwise it zooms on the center of the image.

Manual Zoom:

Enter x/y bounding coordinates to view a specific location. The right and bottom auto-update to the correct dimensions when other values change.

Colors:

Click between black bars to add colors, or click the "X" above bars to remove them. Click a bar and then click the color circle that appears to change its color. Drag bars left/right to adjust the rate a color changes to adjacent ones. Click the color in the box to change the interior color.
